Recipies-World's Best Scrambled Eggs

I adapted this recipe from a book called Sydney Food by Bill Grainger .Ever since tasting these eggs on my 1st visit to Bill's restraunt in Kings Cross, Sydney. I have been after the recipe. I have since transformed it into what I really believe are the scrambled eggs I have ever tasted.

This recipe is what I call a "very special breakfast", just look at the ingredients to see why it has to be tasted to be believed.

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter(10g)
  • 1/3 cup of cream(2 3/4 fl ounces)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives(chopped)

Instructions

  1. Whisk eggs,cream and salt in a bowl
  2. Melt the butter in a non-stick pan over a hih heat(taking care note to burn the butter)
  3. Pour egg mixture into a pan and wit until it starts settling around the edge of the pan(around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatula,bring the mixture into the center as if were an omelet,and let it cook for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold contents in again,leave for 20 seconds and repeat until the eggs are only just done.
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshy miled pepper over the eggs and blend in some chopped fresh chives.

You should only make a maximum of two servings per frying pan
